Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Langpush - AI Language Tutor mobile application (the "Application"), created by Kaindra Rizq Sachio (the "Service Provider"). This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information We Collect

1. Account and Identity Information

When you sign in or create an account, the Application may collect:

  • Email address — if you sign in with email, Google, or Apple
  • Name — if provided by Google or Apple Sign-In
  • Profile photo — if provided by Google Sign-In or other account metadata made available to the Application
  • Internal account identifiers — such as your authentication user ID

Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth. If you sign up with email and password, the Application does not store your plain-text password in its own application database.

2. Conversation and Learning Content

When you use the Application, we may collect content you create or generate through the service, including:

  • Chat messages and conversation history in free talk, roleplay, and topic discussion modes
  • Voice transcripts created from speech-based interactions
  • AI feedback and corrections, including grammar corrections, feedback reports, recap content, and related metrics
  • Vocabulary data such as saved words, definitions, examples, synonyms, CEFR labels, and review status
  • Grammar learning data such as quiz answers, scores, attempts, and draft progress stored on your device
  • Learning workspace data such as the language you are studying

3. Voice, Audio, and Speech Features

If you use microphone or voice features, the Application may process:

  • Microphone input for voice conversation practice
  • Speech-to-text transcripts for dictation and voice interactions
  • Live voice session content when using real-time voice features

The Application requests microphone and speech-recognition permissions for these features. Some speech-to-text features rely on native speech-recognition capabilities provided by your device platform.

4. Usage, Analytics, and Subscription Information

The Application may collect information about how you use the service, including:

  • Practice events such as session starts, session ends, grammar practice events, paywall views, and restore/purchase events
  • Progress and performance data such as review counts, scores, completion state, due counts, and timing metrics
  • Subscription status and purchase-related status information for premium access
  • Analytics identifiers and app metadata used to understand product usage and app performance

If analytics is enabled in the build you are using, the Application uses PostHog for product analytics and session replay. The current configuration masks text inputs, images, and protected replay views.

5. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The Application stores some data locally on your device, including:

  • Authentication session data needed to keep you signed in
  • Conversation preferences such as translation language and text-to-speech preference
  • Your selected learning language
  • Local grammar quiz progress, latest attempts, and in-progress grammar drafts

6. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect for This Application

Based on the current implementation, the Application does not request or use:

  • Precise location data
  • Contacts / phonebook data
  • Camera permissions for app features
  • Third-party advertising SDKs
  • Remarketing services for advertising

The current iOS application bundle includes photo-library usage descriptions. Based on the current implementation we reviewed, the Application does not provide a general user-facing photo feature, but certain native dependencies or platform integrations may still require these declarations to be present in the application metadata.

How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide authentication and keep your account working
  • Deliver AI-powered chat, roleplay, topic discussion, translation, feedback, and live voice features
  • Store and display your conversation history, corrections, reports, vocabulary, and progress
  • Maintain separate learning-language workspaces
  • Support subscription access, restore purchases, and customer subscription management
  • Understand feature usage, diagnose issues, and improve the Application
  • Respond to support requests and account-related communications
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect the security and integrity of the service

Data Retention

Server-side data such as account records, conversations, feedback reports, and vocabulary records may be retained for as long as needed to operate the Application, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Data stored locally on your device, such as preferences and grammar drafts, remains on your device until it is removed by the Application, overwritten, or cleared through app removal or device/app storage controls.

You can initiate account deletion from within the Application. When your account is deleted, the Application is designed to remove the server-side app data associated with that account, subject to any data we may need to retain for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or compliance reasons. If you need help with account or data deletion, you may also contact us at kaindradev@gmail.com.

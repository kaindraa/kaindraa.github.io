Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 19, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Vonote - Vocabulary Tracker app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Kaindra Rizq Sachio (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects certain personally identifiable information when you create an account and use its features. The types of information collected are described below.

Account Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Email address — provided during signup or obtained via third-party authentication (Google or Apple Sign-In)
  • Password — if you register with email/password (stored securely in hashed form; never stored in plain text)
  • Full name and profile photo — obtained from Google or Apple Sign-In, if you choose to authenticate via those providers

User-Generated Content

As you use the Application, we collect content that you create, including:

  • Vocabulary data — words, definitions, examples, synonyms, and antonyms you add to your collections
  • User-authored sentences — sentences you write during practice exercises for AI-powered evaluation

Usage Data

We collect data about how you use the Application to provide progress tracking features:

  • Practice statistics — accuracy, time spent, words practiced, and session history
  • Vocabulary progress — mastery status, practice counts, and streaks

Survey and Preference Data

During onboarding, we collect:

  • Language preference — your target language for vocabulary learning
  • Referral source — how you heard about the Application

The Application does not collect location data, device identifiers for advertising, or use third-party analytics services.

Use of Artificial Intelligence

The Application uses AI technology (powered by OpenAI) to evaluate sentences you write during vocabulary practice exercises. When you submit a sentence for evaluation, the vocabulary word and the sentence you authored are sent to OpenAI's API. Your personal identity information (name, email, user ID) is not included in these requests.

Third-Party Services

The Application relies on the following third-party services to provide its functionality. Additionally, external dictionary services are used for word lookups and autocomplete suggestions; only the words you search are sent to these services (no personal identity data). Each service listed below has its own privacy policy governing how data is handled:

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact the Service Provider at kaindradev@gmail.com so that they will be able to take the necessary actions.
